Lab Media

These lab media resources enable students to expand their scientific horizons beyond the traditional wet lab setting. On the CD you can access Investigations (lab cases that emphasize scientific inquiry) and GraphIt! (interactive graphing activities). Biology Labs On-Line are 12 optional online labs for purchase only and are not included on the CD. LabBench (a series of 12 activities that cover the recommended AP Biology Lab Topics) is accessible through the online website.
