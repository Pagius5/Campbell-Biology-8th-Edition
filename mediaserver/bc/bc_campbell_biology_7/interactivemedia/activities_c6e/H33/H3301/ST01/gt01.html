This activity will help you review nine different phyla of animals-- eight invertebrates and one vertebrate for comparison. Choose the "Quiz" mode to test your understanding of the animal phyla. Choose the "Review" mode to quickly review the correct answers. For better learning, we suggest you challenge yourself with the quiz mode first.

Use the "Animal" pull-down menu at the top to choose an animal to study. Then use each of the other pull-down menus to complete the chart. In "Review" mode, you can compare phyla by quickly switching back and forth between different animals.
