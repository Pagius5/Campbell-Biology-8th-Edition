There are several possible reasons that fire ant quarantines have not worked well. Some of these are:

(a) Many people do not know that there is a quarantine, and they transport plants (and fire ant colonies) when they move to a new house.

(b) A single mated queen can start a new colony, and can be carried from state to state in vehicles without being noticed.

(c) Colonies are underground, and often overlooked, so states that were listed as "uninfected" may already have had colonies of fire ants.
