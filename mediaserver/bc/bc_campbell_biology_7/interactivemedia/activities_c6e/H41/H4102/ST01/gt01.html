Imagine taking a bite of a tasty hamburger. Just thinking about food-- not to mention smelling and tasting it-- makes your mouth water, and also triggers nerve impulses that initiate secretion of gastric juice from glands in the walls of your stomach.

Once food gets to the stomach and small intestine, a number of hormones are secreted which also help regulate digestion.
