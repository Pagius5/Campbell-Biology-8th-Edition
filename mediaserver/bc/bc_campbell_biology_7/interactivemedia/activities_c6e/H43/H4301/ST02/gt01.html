In the nucleated cells of the body, bacterial, viral, or tumor antigens are gathered and presented on the surface by class I MHC proteins. These proteins are recognized by cytotoxic T cell receptors and the cytotoxic T cell surface protein CD8. Cytotoxic T cells are stimulated by contact and by interleukin-2 to release the protein perforin. Click on the perforin vesicles in the cytotoxic T cell to see what happens.
