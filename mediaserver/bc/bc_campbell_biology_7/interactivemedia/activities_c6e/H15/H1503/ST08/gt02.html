Correct. The plant that develops from this zygote would be able to carry out meiosis. The chromosomes could pair, and meiosis would occur normally, producing gametes containing 5 chromosomes apiece. Thus, polyploidy can produce a new plant species— species Z— that is reproductively isolated from the parent species because of their differing numbers of chromosomes.
