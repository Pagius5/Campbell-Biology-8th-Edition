Intertidal Zone

Varying salinity and tides are characteristic of this zone. Fresh water influx from the land and evaporation can lower and raise the salinity. Tides cause periods of flooding and drying to the regions inhabited by organisms in this zone. Only creatures that are tolerant of these extreme changes can live in the coastal zone. The coastal zone is rich in salt-tolerant plants and algae. A wide variety of animals live here feeding off the algae and other animals. Microscopic organisms called plankton are plentiful in this zone. Coral reefs are found in this zone.
