Rules:
The object of the game is to arrange the cards in order in a single stack with the highest level of biological organization on the bottom of the stack.
1. The computer will automatically shuffle and deal the cards.
2. Look at the three columns on the right. If you can, drag and drop cards onto each other so that a smaller level of biological organization is on top of the next larger level (e.g., "Tissues" should be on top of "Organ"). Single cards or stacks of cards can be moved.
3. When an upside-down card is exposed, click on it to turn it face up.
4. Click on the deck on the left to flip cards over. If any card belongs on top of a card on the right, drag it over. When you reach the end of the deck, click the "Flip Deck" button to continue.
5. When an empty column becomes available on the right, any face-up card or stack of cards can be moved to that slot.
6. If you get stuck or just want to play again, click the "New Game" button.
