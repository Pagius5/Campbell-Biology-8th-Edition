This figure shows a cross between a true-breeding green-skinned, black-eyed MendAlien and a white-skinned, orange-eyed MendAlien.

Because the white-skin allele w is recessive to the wild type green-skin allele W, and the orange-eye allele o is recessive to the wild type black-eye allele O, the F1 offspring all have green skin and black eyes.

With the knowledge that the skin-color gene and the eye-color gene assort independently, your task is to continue this cross by interbreeding F1 individuals to produce the F2 generation. Click on the button to make the cross.
