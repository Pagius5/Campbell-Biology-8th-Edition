Here are the results of a cross between two F1 individuals. The total number of offspring in the F2 generation is 527. The observed F2 phenotypic ratio closely matches a 9:3:3:1 ratio.

We can conclude that the genes for the eye-color and skin-color traits assort independently. Orange eyes is recessive to black eyes, and white skin is recessive to green skin.

If we perform a testcross with an F1 individual, (Oo Ww x oo ww), the phenotypic ratio in the offspring will be 1 black eyes, green skin : 1 black eyes, white skin : 1 orange eyes, green skin : 1 orange eyes, white skin.
