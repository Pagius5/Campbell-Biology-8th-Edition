About Tierra del Fuego

Tierra del Fuego is a group of islands at the southern tip of South America. This area is now part of Chile and Argentina.

The name, Tierra del Fuego, (tierra = land; fuego = fire) was given by sailors, who from their passing ships could see the hearth fires of the local Native Americans, the Fuegians. According to Darwin's contemporaries, the Fuegians were one of the most primitive peoples in the world.
