Planning the Voyage

On September 5, 1831 Darwin and FitzRoy met for the first time, and Darwin started learning about the ship that would be his home and the places where he would be traveling. Because the voyage was scheduled to start in October, Darwin immediately started preparations. He would need new clothes, which he would wear for the next two years, and he needed to gather the equipment for his work as a naturalist.
