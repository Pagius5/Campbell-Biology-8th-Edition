About the Andes

In the midst of the austral winter, on June 10, 1834, the H.M.S. Beagle finally entered the Pacific Ocean. It was a stormy and difficult time to be sailing in that area, but eventually she reached Valparaiso, Chile on July 23rd.

Valparaiso, Chile is a coastal city west of the Andes, with a climate that is typical for the coast. Mendoza, Argentina is on the eastern side of the Andes, only three degrees of latitude due east of Valparaiso.
