What you eat can definitely affect your health. A poor or inadequate diet can cause severe health problems. In this activity, you will try to identify particular nutritional disorders from the case histories of ten different people. Click on each of the ten numbers below to read their case histories. Each person may have a healthy diet, or may suffer from malnutrition, undernutrition, obesity, bulimia, or anorexia nervosa. Click on the diagnosis that seems consistent with each case history.
