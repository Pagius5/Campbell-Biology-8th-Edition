1. Which provides the greater amount of Vitamin C: five apples or 1 cup of orange juice? Click on either the apple or the orange juice to answer this question.

Right! Five apples gives you 5 x 20% or 100% of your Daily Value for Vitamin C, but a single cup of orange juice delivers 162% of the Daily Value. Citrus fruits like oranges are good sources of vitamin C. So is broccoli.
