All leaves are not alike across tree species; but a species of tree will always produce leaves that are identical in detail. Thus it is possible to identify a tree by using leaf characteristics.

To do this successfully, you will first learn to recognize some of the features scientists use:

- Leaf Type (Simple or Compound)
- Leaf Venation (Pinnate or Palmate)
- Leaf Arrangement (Alternate, Opposite, or Whorled)

Use the top three buttons at left to review these features. Start with Leaf Type.
