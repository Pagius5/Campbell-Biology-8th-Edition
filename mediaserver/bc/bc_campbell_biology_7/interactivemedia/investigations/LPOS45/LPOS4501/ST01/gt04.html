In Experiment 3, you will investigate the effects of TSH injections on the metabolic rates of the three rats. Select a rat to experiment on first, and then proceed.

1. Click the Reset button in the box labeled Apparatus.

2. Select a rat to test. You will eventually test all three, so it doesn't matter what order you test them in.

3. Your rat should be inside of its cage (not the chamber). Click and drag the syringe labeled TSH over the rat to inject the specimen.

4. Move the rat into the chamber. Repeat the procedure from the previous experiment:

  • Open clamp.
  • Click Weigh button.
  • Set timer for 1 minute.
  • Close clamp.
  • Click Start button.
  • When timer stops, open clamp.
  • Inject oxygen until fluid is level.
  • Click Record Data button.

5. Reset your experiment and click Clean on the top left of the cage to cleanse the rat of all traces of TSH.

Repeat this procedure for the remaining rats. Then answer Questions 12-17.
