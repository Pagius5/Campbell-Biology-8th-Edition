TEST OF HYPOTHESIS I, an experiment:

1. Manure with Pilobolus developing on it is placed in four opaque dishes.
2. Two of the dishes are covered with transparent lids.
3. Each of the other two dishes is covered with an opaque lid that has one transparent window that is 1 square cm.
4. The dishes are placed side by side, and a light shines directly down on them.

Click on the dishes to observe the patterns of sporangia later in the day.
