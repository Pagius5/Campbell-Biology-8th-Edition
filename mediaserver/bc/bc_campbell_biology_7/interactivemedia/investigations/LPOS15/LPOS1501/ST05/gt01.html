You've seen how you breed flies in the laboratory and analyze the results of the breeding through the F2 generation. The next steps will help you understand the patterns of inheritance in your fly populations.
One way to discover patterns of inheritance is by working backward. In other words, you determine the genotype of the original parental generation by careful analysis of the F1 and F2 generations.
