You now know that the aggregation molecule is a type of nucleotide. However, it can be one or more of many nucleotides used in cell communication. Your next task is to perform an exact identification of the aggregation molecule.

Liquid chromatography is one type of analytical method for determining the nature of a chemical. In this last part of the experiment you will place a sample of the aggregate molecule into the chromatography apparatus. You will then compare it with a series of known nucleotides.
