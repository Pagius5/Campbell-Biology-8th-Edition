Stage four of the hypothesis for the origin of life involves the formation of pre-cells. The conditions for creating cell-like structures are different from the conditions in the prior three stages. Researchers believe that the components of the prior stages, now called a primordial soup, sat for many years in shallow pools and on the surface of wet soil. Experiments modeling these conditions have led to conclusions that support the current hypotheses.
