We are now at the scale of 10 mm (1 cm, about 3/8 inch).

Many insect sizes are on the order of 10 mm. For example, most ants range from 0.2-20 mm. The ant shown here has a body length of about 7-12 mm.

Insects are the most diverse group of living organisms. There are over 600,000 species of beetles alone, and many millions of insect species.
