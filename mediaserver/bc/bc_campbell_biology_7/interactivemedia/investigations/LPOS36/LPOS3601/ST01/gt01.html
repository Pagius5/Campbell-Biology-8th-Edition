Water enters a plant through the root hairs, passes through the tissues of the root into the xylem, and travels up through the xylem vessels into the leaves. Transpiration, the evaporation of water from the leaves, is the major factor that pulls the water up through the plant. In this activity you will be calculating rates of transpiration
