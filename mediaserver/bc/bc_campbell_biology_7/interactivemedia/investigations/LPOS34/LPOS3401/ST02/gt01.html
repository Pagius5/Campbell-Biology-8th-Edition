The skeleton of Archaeopteryx is strikingly similar to a group of small, carnivorous, bipedal dinosaurs called coelurosaurs. In fact, skeletons and fossils of small coelurosaur dinosaurs like Compsognathus and Bambiraptor (named for the deer in the Disney movie for its diminutive size) shown at left have even been confused with Archaeopteryx. The Velociraptor from Michael Crichton's Jurassic Park was such a dinosaur too. The abundant similarities and the age of the Archaeopteryx fossils strongly support the idea that birds evolved from dinosaur coelurosaurs, sometime around the late Jurassic Period.
