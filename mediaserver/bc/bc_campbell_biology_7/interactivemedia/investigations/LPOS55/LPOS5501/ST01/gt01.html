Concepts of the theory of island biogeography are applicable not only to oceanic islands but also to very isolated ecosystems. For example, colonization of mountain lakes, natural areas within agricultural fields, and clear-cut areas generally follow patterns that would be predicted by island biogeography. The theory of island biogeography has also been used to design nature preserves.
