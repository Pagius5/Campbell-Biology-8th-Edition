We are looking up the back of the embryo, with what will become the head at the top. The blastopore later becomes the anus. Under the dorsal surface, from head to tail, a rod of mesoderm called the notochord is forming. Later, it will be replaced by the vertebral column. Above the notochord, the ectoderm thickens, forming a neural plate, bounded by two neural folds. This is the beginning of the neurulation, the formation of the nervous system.
