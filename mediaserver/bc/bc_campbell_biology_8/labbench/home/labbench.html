LabBench


This set of activities covers many of the laboratory topics and techniques used in an introductory biology course.
Animations and interactive questions help you connect laboratory procedures to the biological principles you are studying.
Each laboratory module includes self-assessment questions and a final quiz.



Instructors: You can access the answers to LabBench quizzes in the 'For Instructors' site. You must have an instructor's access code to enter the Instructor Resources section.

* Note: Some of the functionality in LabBench requires that cookies are enabled in your browser.
