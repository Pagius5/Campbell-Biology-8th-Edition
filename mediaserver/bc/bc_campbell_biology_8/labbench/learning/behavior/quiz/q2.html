Students placed five pillbugs on the dry side of a choice chamber and five pillbugs on the wet side. They collected data as to the number on each side every 30 seconds for 10 minutes. After 6 minutes, eight or nine pillbugs were continually on the wet side of the chamber, and several were under the filter paper. Which of the following is NOT a reasonable conclusion from these results?

a. It takes the pillbugs several minutes to explore their surroundings and select a preferred habitat.
b. Pillbugs prefer a moist environment.
c. Pillbugs prefer a dark environment.
d. Pillbugs may find chemicals in dry filter paper irritating.
e. Pillbugs demonstrate no significant habitat preference.
