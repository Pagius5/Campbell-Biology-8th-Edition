Enzyme Catalysis
Design of the Experiment

In this experiment, you will investigate the rate at which the enzyme catalase converts substrate to product. You will allow catalase to react with hydrogen peroxide for varying amounts of time and then stop the reactions by adding H2SO4.


You must wear goggles for this laboratory!
To determine the amount of hydrogen peroxide that remains after the reaction, you will do a titration with KMnO4. In such a titration, you slowly add a chemical (KMnO4) that will cause a color change until a target color is achieved.
