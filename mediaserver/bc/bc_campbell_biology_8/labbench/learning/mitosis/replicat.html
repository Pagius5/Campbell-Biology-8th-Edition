Mitosis & Meiosis
Interphase

Most of the cell cycle is spent in interphase. During interphase the cell is growing and metabolic activity is very high. Toward the end of interphase, new DNA is synthesized and the chromosomes are replicated. Each chromosome of the homologous pair is then composed of two sister chromatids.

To see homologous pairs, go to Homologous Chromosomes.
